Frequently Asked Questions About Beard Trimming & Shaping

How much does a beard trim and shape cost, and how long does it take?
Our basic beard trim and shape service is $35. For fuller beards or more detailed styling work, the price ranges from $40-50. A standard beard service typically takes 30-35 minutes, while more intricate styling can take up to 45 minutes. We recommend booking extra time if you'd like a consultation about beard growth patterns or styling options specific to your face shape.
Do I need to book an appointment or do you take walk-ins?
While we do accept walk-ins when possible, we strongly recommend booking an appointment, especially during peak hours (weekdays 4-7pm and weekends). You can book online through our website or call us at our East Village location. We typically can accommodate same-day appointments if you call before 2pm on weekdays.
What's included in your beard trimming service?
Our beard service includes a consultation to discuss your desired shape and style, hot towel treatment, trimming with both scissors and clippers, precise shape definition, neckline cleanup, and finishing with beard oil. We also provide advice on maintaining your beard between visits and product recommendations tailored to your beard type and texture.
I've never had my beard professionally trimmed before - how do I prepare?
Come in with your beard clean and dry. It's helpful to have reference photos of styles you like, but don't trim your beard yourself beforehand. Ideally, let it grow for at least 2-3 weeks before your visit. During your first appointment, we'll assess your growth pattern and face shape to recommend styles that will work best for you. Don't worry if you're unsure - our barbers are experienced in working with first-timers.
Do you offer any guarantees on your beard trimming services?
Yes, we stand behind our work. If you're not completely satisfied with your beard trim, come back within 48 hours and we'll adjust it free of charge. Our barbers are all licensed by New York State and have specialized training in beard care and styling. We also guarantee that we only use sterilized equipment and follow all NYC health department regulations for barbershops.
