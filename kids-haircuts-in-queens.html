Frequently Asked Questions About Kids' Haircuts

Do I need an appointment for my child's haircut or do you take walk-ins?
We accept both appointments and walk-ins at B & H Barber Shop, but we strongly recommend booking appointments for children's haircuts, especially during after-school hours (3-6 PM) and weekends when we're busiest. You can book by calling us at our East Village location or using our online booking system. Walk-ins are welcome, but please note that wait times can be 30-45 minutes during peak hours.
How much do children's haircuts cost and what ages do you serve?
We charge $25 for children's haircuts (ages 12 and under). For toddlers (ages 1-3), the price is the same but includes extra time and attention. We have special booster seats and child-friendly cape options for younger kids. All our prices include a wash and style. We welcome children of all ages, but those under 3 must be accompanied by a parent who can help keep them steady during the cut.
How do you handle children who are nervous about getting haircuts?
Our barbers are specially trained to work with anxious children. We have TVs at each station playing kid-friendly content, lollipops for after the cut, and special character-themed capes. We take extra time to explain the process to nervous kids and let them touch (safe) tools before starting. Parents are welcome to stay right beside their child, and we can do the cut in stages with breaks if needed. Our goal is to make it a positive experience.
What's included in a typical kids' haircut at B & H Barber Shop?
Each kids' haircut includes a consultation with parent and child, shampoo (if desired), the cut itself, and styling. We use professional-grade tools and products safe for children's sensitive skin. The entire service typically takes 20-30 minutes, depending on the hairstyle and child's comfort level. We finish with a light spritz of water and styling, plus a quick cleanup around the neck and ears. Every cut includes our signature neck brush cleanup and final inspection with parents.
Do you have experience with special needs children or sensory issues?
Yes, we have several barbers who are experienced in cutting hair for children with special needs and sensory sensitivities. We offer quieter appointment times (usually first thing in the morning), can dim lights if needed, and have noise-canceling headphones available. Please let us know about any specific requirements when booking, and we'll match you with our most experienced barber for your child's needs. We're happy to do the cut in stages or adapt our process to make your child comfortable.
