Frequently Asked Questions About Hot Towel Shaves

How much does a hot towel shave cost at B & H Barber Shop?
Our traditional hot towel shave service is $35. This includes a pre-shave oil treatment, two passes with a straight razor, multiple hot towel applications, and a soothing aftershave balm. For first-time clients, we recommend allowing 45 minutes for the full experience.
Do I need to make an appointment for a hot towel shave?
While we accept walk-ins, we strongly recommend booking an appointment for hot towel shaves, especially during peak hours (weekday afternoons and Saturdays). You can call us at our Jersey City location to schedule, or book through our website. We typically require 24-hour notice for cancellations.
How often should I get a professional hot towel shave?
For optimal results, we recommend getting a professional hot towel shave every 2-3 weeks. However, this varies based on your beard growth rate and skin sensitivity. Our experienced barbers can assess your specific needs during your visit and recommend a personalized schedule.
Is it safe to get a hot towel shave if I have sensitive skin?
Yes, our barbers are trained to work with all skin types. Before beginning, we'll discuss any skin sensitivities or concerns you have. We use premium, hypoallergenic products and can adjust our technique accordingly. If you have specific skin conditions, please let us know when booking your appointment.
What's included in your hot towel shave service?
Our complete hot towel shave service includes: 1) Facial cleansing and hot towel preparation, 2) Pre-shave oil application, 3) Hot lather application, 4) First pass with a straight razor, 5) Additional hot towel and lather application, 6) Second pass against the grain, 7) Final hot towel treatment, 8) Aftershave balm and moisturizer. The entire process takes approximately 30-40 minutes.
